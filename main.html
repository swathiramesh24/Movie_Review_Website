"Tamasha" is not just a love story; it is a deep dive into the struggle between societal expectations and personal passion. The film follows Ved (Ranbir Kapoor), who, as a child, is fascinated by storytelling but grows up to live a monotonous life, to conform to societal norms. His encounter with Tara (Deepika Padukone) in Corsica reignites his lost spark, but she sees a completely different Ved—one trapped in a robotic corporate routine.

"La La Land" is a mesmerizing blend of romance, jazz, and dreams, set against the backdrop of Los Angeles. The film follows Sebastian (Ryan Gosling), a passionate jazz musician, and Mia (Emma Stone), an aspiring actress, as they fall in love while chasing their dreams. However, as success comes knocking, their relationship faces challenges, leading to an emotional and bittersweet conclusion.

"This is not a love story. This is a story about love." The film follows Tom (Joseph Gordon-Levitt), a hopeless romantic who falls for Summer (Zooey Deschanel), a free-spirited woman who doesn’t believe in true love. Told in a nonlinear format, the movie jumps between the highs and lows of their 500-day relationship, making the audience feel the excitement, heartbreak, and nostalgia right alongside Tom.

"Hi Nanna" is an emotional rollercoaster that blends love, fatherhood, and relationships into a heartfelt drama. The story follows Viraj (Nani), a single father raising his daughter Mahi (Kiara Khanna). His life takes an unexpected turn when Yashna (Mrunal Thakur) enters their world, unraveling emotions, secrets, and past wounds.

"Interstellar" is not just a sci-fi film; it is an emotional and intellectual masterpiece that explores space, time, and the power of human connection. The story follows Cooper (Matthew McConaughey), a former NASA pilot who embarks on a mission to find a new habitable planet. However, this journey takes him across distant galaxies,even the depths of a black hole, forcing him to make heartbreaking choices.

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" (YJHD) is a film that beautifully captures love, friendship, dreams, and the bittersweet transition from youth to adulthood. The story revolves around Bunny (Ranbir Kapoor), a free-spirited traveler and Naina (Deepika Padukone), a shy and studious girl who steps out for a life-changing trip. As they meet, part ways, and reunite, the film explores self-discovery, love, and the importance of friendships.
